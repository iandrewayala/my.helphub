Our List of Charities:

The Water Charity

In this charity, we are focused on helping out the people with no access to clean water. This non-profit organization was formed in 2006 with the goal of providing people in developing nations with the healthy drinking water. As of 2019, this organization has funded over $370 million.

Feeding America

In this charity, we tend to look out for the needy portion of the population. We use the donations you give to make an impact on any generation in need of these things. Formed in 1982 by the Rotary Club of Milwaukee, the main mission is to defeat America's hunger problem.

The World Wildlife Fund

The main goal of this non-profit charity is to help educate people about the ongoing troubles that occur in the wilderness, donate money to help save any endangered species, and adopt these poor creatures. The charity provides users with toolkits which include a reading, a plan to save species, and more resources.
